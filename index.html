About Us

We are a small non-profit organisation and a community creating free and tested content for Minecraft: Bedrock Edition. Our packs are kept supported as long as the technical state of the game allows us to, meaning when any breaking changes occur we fix our packs to be working and updated for the latest version of the game. Our goal is to make you enjoy the experiences we create without having to pay anything.
